If endo or other chronic illness is a factor for you and you want

relatable financial advice to help wade through funding options and

still living your life to the fullest, reach out.

What will you be dealing with, and how much will it cost? As with

any stage in your life, once you decide to have a family (refer back

to chapter  2), you’ll need to plan. Depending on your income

and budget, think about whether you’ll save for this in an extra

‘squirrel account: baby fund’, or whether you can ‘cash flow it’

with your ongoing income from parental leave (from work and/

or the government’s Parental Leave Pay) or sufficient income

from a spouse or partner. I’ve outlined some of the potential

costs below.

It may be easy to chunk this planning into the following stages to

help both financially and emotionally, as you think it through:

•

trying to conceive

•

pregnancy

•

birth and setup

•

time off / parental leave

•

back to work

•

child care

•

schooling.
