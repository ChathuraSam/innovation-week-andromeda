Step 1: Deal with ‘you’

It can be uncomfortable imagining ourselves in a better position.

Over time the inevitable hurdles of life can chip away at our

confidence and ability to dream.

The good news is that this is temporary and reversible.

Make a short list of what you would call your strengths and

weaknesses; aim for three of each. Start with strengths, as these

are usually easier to bring to the surface. With weaknesses, think

about recent times you’ve failed to achieve something. Maybe you

thought about starting a business but didn’t get it off the ground, or

you failed to reach a weight-­

loss goal or a savings target for a holiday
