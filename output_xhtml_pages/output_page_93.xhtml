Sufficiently Decluttered

By the end of this chapter:

■

■

As in every other area of life that matters, you understand

the importance of clarity and focus when dealing with

your finances.

■

■

You have a list of all your debts and have begun to

snowball or avalanche these the hell out of your

life forever!

■

■

You’ve completed your budget with your net income and

every possible weekly, monthly and annual expense, as

well as any additional spending in line with your new list of

goals from chapter 1.

exactly how much money you need in this account.
