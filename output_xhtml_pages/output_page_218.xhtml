Remember, achieving Sufficient is not a goal to apply only to money.

I want you to create abundant wealth, yes, but the vision I have for

you includes the time and the health to enjoy it. Imagine a world in

which money isn’t a constraint on your time, and your time isn’t a

constraint to your health.

Now imagine the new feeling you’d have if this was your reality

and how far that feeling is from that dismal message of ‘Insufficient

Funds’ on the ATM slip. Let go of that image, hold onto that new

feeling and go smash it!
