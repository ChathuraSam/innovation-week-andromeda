Can I start with zero dollars?

Starting with $0 is perfect. You’re not alone. It’s the structure that’s

so crucial here, not the amounts in it. If close to $0 is your current

reality, this new system for your money will help ensure you’re

giving yourself every chance of future financial success, even if

you’re not feeling it right now.
