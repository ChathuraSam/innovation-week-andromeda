Buying happiness in Kuwait, and why you need

challenge in your life

One time at a conference I had the pleasure of hearing a keynote

from peak performance researcher Adam Fraser.

During his talk Dr Fraser spoke about how he was hired by the

Kuwaiti government to help make the country happier. The talk

had a number of focus points but this one stuck out, so I did

some digging.

It turns out that Kuwait has had an amazing history of resilience

and innovation. At the same time, they have amassed great wealth

from their three main industries, banking, telco and of course oil.

It’s well understood that in recent times their solution to almost

every problem has been to throw cash at it, the prime focus being

keeping the Kuwaiti nationals happy. Citizens enjoy many benefits

(not available to foreign nationals), including interest-­

free housing
