So is it a no-­

brainer to dabble in crypto? Here are some brief points

to consider when determining if cryptocurrency investment makes

sense on your path to Sufficient Funds.

Benefits of investing in cryptocurrency may include:

•

Bright shiny objects! It’s still relatively new. It’s a novelty.

It feels exciting. If this speaks to you, and motivates you

to save, then invest wisely, this could be a part of your

strategy. Sure, crypto has been around for a while now, but

in comparison to traditional investment options, it’s still

in its infancy. Making the move to invest now could mean

you’re jumping in on the first floor (the ground floor would

have been 2013!), before potential future growth, both in

profit and popularity. This is by no means guaranteed, but if

you are attracted to things that might go boom, and have a

healthy buffer to allow for volatility, it may be worth a crack.
