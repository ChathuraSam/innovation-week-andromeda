Fuck F.I.R.E. — why retirement is

NOT the goal

You may have heard of the FIRE movement? Investopedia describes

it as follows:

a movement dedicated to a program of extreme savings … By

dedicating up to 70% of income to savings, followers of the FIRE

movement may eventually be able to quit their jobs and live solely

off small withdrawals from their portfolios decades before the

conventional retirement age of 65.
